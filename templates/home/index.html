Bringing People Together

Center for Performance Arts and Learning strives to bring people together through dance,music,arts and culture.

We want to be your Dance Family.

Center for Performance arts and Learning has provided a quality performing arts education for people of all ages and skill levels, in a friendly environment. Their qualified instructors teach not only technique, but they help instill life skills that can last a lifetime.

Learn,Collaborate and Thrive

We here at Cen4PAL provide a center "A Room" to Learn,Reflect,Collaborate and Thrive .

Our Mission

We Strive to bring people together through dance,music,arts and culture.Creating and fostering an all-inclusive environment dedicated to building a strong culture of acceptance,empowerment and collaboration,spanning diverse cultural communities.
